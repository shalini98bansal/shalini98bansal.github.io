01 / About

I'm a mechanical engineer at Haas Automation in Oxnard — designing complex mechanical systems, owning projects from inherited concepts through DFM/DFA, cross-functional reviews, and production release. I've also represented Haas at the Dealer Meeting, presenting conveyor design and application guidance that directly influenced purchasing decisions across dealer networks.

Before moving into product design, I spent nearly four years on the machine test floor — machining test parts, writing test plans, automating 200+ regression tests in Python, and mentoring engineers on CNC workflows. Earlier in my career I performed aerospace FEA at Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, built a steerable robotic catheter prototype at UC Riverside, and designed the handlebar mechanism for a human-powered recumbent racer that I also drove in competition.

Outside of Haas, I volunteer at FIRST Robotics events, representing the Gene Haas Foundation and introducing students to CNC manufacturing. I'm also finishing an M.S. in Computer Science, because building things well means understanding everything around them, not just the parts you can touch.
